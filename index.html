Howdy there! My name is Jeff, and I love teaching. I believe that students deserve the opportunity to do whatever it is that motivates them, and so as a teacher, it's my responsibility to make sure that learning stays delicately balanced exactly halfway between boredom and panic: in the learning zone.

One of the most important pieces of writing I've ever read is the Doodle Alley comic called Be Friends with Failure" because it articulates an important truth about learning. In short, there is no way to learn without encountering failure. Success is a sign you're ready to do something new, and failure is a sign you're already doing something new.
